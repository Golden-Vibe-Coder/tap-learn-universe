# Game Rules

## Target Audience

Ages 3 and up. No reading required — the entire experience is visual and touch-based.

---

## Mode 1 — Trace the Number

### Objective

Trace each number with your finger to make it light up, then move on to the next one.

### How It Works

1. A large number appears centered on the screen in bubble-letter style.
2. Light gray dots are spaced in a single line along the number's stroke, showing exactly where to trace.
3. A golden guide arrow floats just ahead of the player's position along the path, indicating the correct tracing direction. For numbers with multiple digits (e.g. 10), each digit shows its own independent guide arrow.
4. The player presses and drags their finger over the number.
5. Each dot within reach of the finger **pops and disappears**.
6. A progress bar at the bottom of the screen fills as dots are cleared.
7. When **100%** of the dots are cleared, the number is considered complete.
8. A short celebration plays (confetti burst + cheerful sparkle sound + congratulatory message).
9. The next number appears automatically.

### Progression

| Stage | Numbers shown |
|---|---|
| Start | 1 |
| Continues | 2, 3, 4, … |
| No upper limit | The sequence continues as long as the player keeps playing |

Numbers increase by 1 each time. There is no failure state — the player simply keeps tracing until enough dots are cleared.

### Completion Rules

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Dots required to complete | 100% cleared |
| Celebration duration | ~1.8 seconds |
| Transition | Automatic after celebration ends |

### Controls

| Device | Action |
|---|---|
| Touch screen | Press and drag finger over the number |
| Mouse (desktop) | Hold left button and drag |

### Visual Feedback

- **Background** — colorful candy-store scene (shelves of gumball jars, lollipops, and candy canes) with a hanging "Candy Shop" sign and bunting flags
- **Hint label** — "Trace the number!" appears below the bunting at the start of each number
- **Dots** — light gray circles on the number's strokes; pop away when touched
- **Guide arrow** — a pulsing gold arrowhead tracks just ahead of the tracing frontier for each digit, pointing in the correct direction of travel
- **Trail** — a soft white glow follows the finger while tracing
- **Progress bar** — fills from left to right at the bottom of the screen
- **Celebration** — colorful confetti particles burst from center; a congratulatory message scales in and out, accompanied by a sparkle sound effect
