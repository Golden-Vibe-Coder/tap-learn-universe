# Game Rules

## Target Audience

Ages 3 and up. No reading required — the entire experience is visual and touch-based.

---

## Mode 1 — Trace the Number

### Objective

Trace each number with your finger to make it light up, then move on to the next one.

### How It Works

1. A large number appears centered on the screen in bubble-letter style.
2. Light gray dots are spaced in a single line along the number's stroke, showing exactly where to trace.
3. A golden guide arrow floats just ahead of the player's position along the path, indicating the correct tracing direction. For numbers with multiple digits (e.g. 10), each digit shows its own independent guide arrow.
4. The player presses and drags their finger over the number.
5. Each dot within reach of the finger **pops and disappears**.
6. A progress bar at the bottom of the screen fills as dots are cleared.
7. When **100%** of the dots are cleared, the number is considered complete.
8. A short celebration plays (confetti burst + cheerful sparkle sound + congratulatory message).
9. The next number appears automatically.

### Progression

| Stage | Numbers shown |
|---|---|
| Start | 1 |
| Continues | 2, 3, 4, … |
| No upper limit | The sequence continues as long as the player keeps playing |

Numbers increase by 1 each time. There is no failure state — the player simply keeps tracing until enough dots are cleared.

### Completion Rules

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Dots required to complete | 100% cleared |
| Celebration duration | ~1.8 seconds |
| Transition | Automatic after celebration ends |

### Controls

| Device | Action |
|---|---|
| Touch screen | Press and drag finger over the number |
| Mouse (desktop) | Hold left button and drag |

### Visual Feedback

- **Background** — colorful candy-store scene (shelves of gumball jars, lollipops, jelly beans, gummy bears, ribbon candy, and open tubs) with a hanging pastel-pink "Candy Shop" sign and bunting flags
- **Hint label** — "Trace the number!" appears below the bunting at the start of each number
- **Dots** — light gray circles on the number's strokes; pop away when touched
- **Guide arrow** — a pulsing gold arrowhead tracks just ahead of the tracing frontier for each digit, pointing in the correct direction of travel
- **Trail** — a soft white glow follows the finger while tracing
- **Progress bar** — fills from left to right at the bottom of the screen
- **Celebration** — colorful confetti particles burst from center; a congratulatory message scales in and out, accompanied by a sparkle sound effect

## Mode 2 — Drag the Letter

### Objective

Match the first letter of a word by dragging and dropping it into the correct slot.

### How It Works

1. A picture of a simple object appears in the center of the screen (e.g. a car, a bee, a sun).
2. Below the picture, the object's word is displayed with the **first letter missing** — shown as a dashed box with a "?" placeholder.
3. The remaining letters of the word are shown in solid boxes beside the blank slot.
4. A large, green **letter tile** sits at the bottom of the screen, displaying the correct first letter.
5. The player taps and holds the tile, then drags it across the screen.
6. If the tile is dropped **over the blank slot**, it snaps into place, the slot turns green, and a celebration plays (confetti + sparkle sound + congratulatory message).
7. If the tile is dropped **away from the slot**, it snaps back to its starting position so the player can try again.
8. After the celebration, the next word appears automatically.

### Word List

Words are shuffled randomly each session. Current set:

| Letter | Word | Picture |
|---|---|---|
| A | ANT | Ant with segmented body and antennae |
| B | BEE | Bee with striped body and wings |
| C | CAR | Red car in side profile |
| D | DOG | Dog face with floppy ears |
| E | EGG | Cracked egg |
| F | FISH | Orange fish with fins |
| H | HAT | Red hat with brim and band |
| K | KEY | Gold key with ring handle |
| M | MOON | Yellow crescent moon with stars |
| O | OWL | Brown owl with large eyes |
| P | PIG | Pink pig face |
| S | SUN | Yellow sun with rays and smile |

### Progression

Words cycle through the shuffled set. Once all 12 words have been shown, the set re-shuffles and repeats. There is no failure state — the player simply keeps trying until the letter lands in the slot.

### Controls

| Device | Action |
|---|---|
| Touch screen | Tap and hold the letter tile, drag to the slot, release |
| Mouse (desktop) | Click and hold the tile, drag to the slot, release |

### Visual Feedback

- **Background** — pre-K classroom scene with green chalkboard, colorful A–Z alphabet frieze, bulletin board with pushpins, cubbies, light blue lower wall, and wooden plank flooring
- **Hint text** — "Drag the letter!" appears on the chalkboard
- **Picture card** — a white rounded card behind the object illustration
- **Word row** — first slot is a dashed outline (empty) or green fill (placed); remaining letters in solid white boxes
- **Letter tile** — large green rounded square with the letter in white; pulses gently to attract attention
- **Snap back** — tile animates back to its home position when dropped outside the target
- **Celebration** — confetti particles + congratulatory message (same style as Numbers mode), accompanied by the sparkle sound effect
